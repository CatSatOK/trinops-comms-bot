Bookings & services

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Friday 08:00 to 18:00 and Saturday 09:00 to 13:00. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

What areas do you cover?

We cover Example Town and everywhere within roughly 40 km of it. Not sure if that includes you? Send us your postcode and we will confirm.

How do I get a quote?

Use the contact form on our website or email hello@example.com with a short description of the job. We reply with a fixed quote within one working day.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major debit and credit cards, bank transfer and Apple Pay. Payment is due within 14 days of the invoice date.

Do you offer emergency call-outs?

Yes. Emergency call-outs are available 24/7 and carry a fixed surcharge on top of our standard rates. Call the emergency line listed on your booking confirmation.

Appointments & guarantees

How quickly can you schedule a visit?

Routine visits are usually scheduled within three working days. You will get a confirmation email with the date and an arrival window.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule free of charge up to 24 hours before the appointment. Later cancellations are charged at 50% of the booked rate.

Are your engineers certified?

Yes. Every engineer is fully certified, insured and DBS-checked, and we re-certify annually.

Do you provide a warranty on your work?

All work comes with a 12-month parts and labour warranty. If anything we fixed fails within that period, we return and put it right at no cost.

How can I track my appointment?

After booking you receive a confirmation email with a tracking link, plus a reminder the day before and a message when the engineer is on the way.
